MISSION STATEMENT


We are a group of people that are connected by our common passions so that we may inspire others through
learning and teaching. Growth is a constant part of what we do so that our family will learn to pursue excellence
and maintain our synergy. Confidence is gained through humility, dedication, and discipline in everything that we
pursue. Our goal is to reaffirm the importance of freedom so that expression is the purest representation of ourselves.



FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


11th Generation FAQs


Will you be having auditions for 11th Generation?


For this 2020 Fall Quarter, we will not be having auditions for 11th Generation due to Covid-19. We hope to continue updating all interested dancers throughout the 2020-2021 year about our plans for the next season.

General FAQs


Q: Do you need dance experience to audition?


A: URMO welcomes dancers of all experience levels, even those with no prior dance experience.

Q: Where and when are practices?


    When: Sundays @ 7PM
              Tuesdays & Wednesdays @ 9PM

    Where: UCI Campus

Q: What performances does URMO participate in?


A: URMO performs in various UCI events and competes in competitions such as TET Festival and OC Night Market.

Q: What dance styles does URMO focus on?


A: URMO focuses on a variety of dance styles, including hip-hop, hype, R&B, iso, femme, etc. We expose our dancers to several different styles to encourage their growth and versatility.

Q: How do auditions work?


A: On audition day, after learning 3 pieces at our audition workshops, auditionees will perform one run through of the 3 pieces taught. There are no group rounds, but there will be an optional freestyle cypher at the end of auditions. (This is your opportunity to show us your talents!)
